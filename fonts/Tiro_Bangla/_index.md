# Tiro Bangla

Tiro Bangla is an elegant typeface crafted by renowned designers John Hudson and Fiona Ross, with valuable assistance from Neelakash Kshetrimayum. The font's heritage traces back to a typeface specifically designed for the prestigious Murty Classical Library of India book series.

## Font Information

- **Font Name:** Tiro Bangla
- **Designer:** John Hudson & Fiona Ross
- **Version:** 1.52
